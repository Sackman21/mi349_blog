The Last of Us

The Last of Us is a video game that holds a special place in my heart. The games takes place in the year 2033, twenty years after a zombie outbreak caused by a fungal infection. Throughout the game you play as a man named Joel Miller and he is tasked with protecting and taking a girl named Ellie across the country as she seems to be immune from the infection and she could be used to make a cure.
